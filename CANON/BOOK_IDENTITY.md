# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 081
**Title:** The Luggage Room
**Slug:** luggage-room
**Category:** Circumstantial/Random

## The Death (One Sentence)
A perfectionist traveler, locked in the overcrowded luggage storage room during a busy check-in rush, slowly suffocates as suitcases shift and compress the diminishing air space.

## Tagline
"She had it all packed. Except an exit strategy."

## Themes
1. The false sense of security in organized spaces (satirizes the belief that being prepared prevents disaster)
2. The dehumanization of budget travelers (treated as cargo, dies among cargo)
3. How easily one person can be overlooked in a crowd

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 35%
- Horror: 45%
- Satire: 20%

## Target Reader
Anyone who has ever trusted a hostel's luggage storage, or obsessively organized their belongings while traveling. The hyper-prepared planner who thinks they've accounted for everything.

## Comparison Titles
- Buried (2010) meets The Terminal (2004)
